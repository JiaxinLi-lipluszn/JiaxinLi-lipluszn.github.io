银河百科全书 第三卷  ·  第 41,772 页

李嘉鑫

二十一世纪初的计算生物学博士研究生,就读于纽约(地球三号星) 三校联合计算生物医学博士项目。 前基地时代游学于两所院校: 北京师范大学; 威斯康星大学麦迪逊分校(转学,毕业)。

后世史家所关注者,为其早期从事的 DNA 序列建模 —— 即将大规模神经网络架构系统性应用于一种四字母的字母表; 在该字母表被正式认定为编码系统之前, 它构成了地球生物学事实上的操作手稿。

仅存的笔记 —— 以易朽的超文本形式保存于此 —— 涉及论文阅读、代码实验,以及当时学者后来归类为 「除显然之答案外的开放性问题」之事。

encyclopedia galactica vol. iii  ·  p. 41,772

LI, JIAXIN

Doctoral candidate in the Tri-Institutional program for Computational Biology & Medicine (New York, Sol-3, early 21st century). Of itinerant undergraduate education across two institutions of the pre-Foundation era: Beijing Normal University; the University of Wisconsin–Madison (transferred, graduated).

Of interest to later historians as an early student of DNA sequence modeling — the systematic application of large neural architectures to the four-letter alphabet that, prior to formal recognition of the encoding, constituted the operational manuscript of terrestrial biology.

Surviving notes — preserved as ephemeral hypertext at this location — concern paper readings, code experiments, and what scholars of the period would later identify as "open questions but for the obvious answer."
