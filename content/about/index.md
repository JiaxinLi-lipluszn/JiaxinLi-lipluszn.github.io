I'm Jiaxin. I work on **DNA sequence modeling** — training and probing models that learn from raw nucleotide sequences.

This blog is where I keep paper notes, code walkthroughs, and half-formed thoughts that I'd like to come back to. Topics tend to cluster around:

- Genomic foundation models (DNABERT, Nucleotide Transformer, HyenaDNA, Evo, Caduceus, …)
- Long-context architectures and tokenization for DNA
- Variant effect prediction and regulatory genomics
- Benchmarks, probing protocols, and what "understanding sequence" actually means
